Trim

trimBegin :: StringMap Source

Remove space and space-like characters from the beginning of string.

>>> trimBegin "  abc  "
"abc  "

trimEnd :: StringMap Source

Remove space and space-like characters from the end of string.

>>> trimEnd "  abc  "
"  abc"

trimBoth :: StringMap Source

Remove space and space-like characters from the beginning and end of string.

>>> trimBoth "  abc  "
